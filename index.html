Before You Apply

Requirements to Become an ESL Teacher for Chinese Students

These are the standard requirements most Classin-based ESL companies require. Brian will help you prepare all of them.

💻

Laptop or Desktop

Minimum Intel Core i3 processor or equivalent. Classin runs best on a dedicated computer — not mobile.

📶

Internet Speed

At least 10 Mbps upload AND download. Stable, wired connection recommended for zero lag during class.

📜

TEFL Certificate

Most companies require a TEFL/TESOL certificate. Don't have one yet? Brian can guide you to the right affordable option.

🎬

Video Introduction

A professional, engaging intro video is required by most platforms. Brian coaches you on how to create one that stands out.
