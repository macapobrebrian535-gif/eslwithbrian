Before You Apply

Requirements to Teach Chinese Students on Classin

Standard requirements most Classin-based ESL companies require. Brian helps you prepare every single one.

💻

Laptop or Desktop

Minimum Intel Core i3 or equivalent. Classin runs best on a dedicated computer — not mobile.

📶

Internet Speed

At least 10 Mbps upload AND download. Stable wired connection recommended for zero lag.

📜

TEFL Certificate

Most companies require TEFL/TESOL. Don't have one? Brian guides you to the right affordable option.

🎬

Video Introduction

A professional, engaging intro video is required. Brian coaches you on creating one that stands out.
